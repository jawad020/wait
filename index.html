Parameters Used to Calculate Risk

These 8 parameters are monitored continuously:

1. Altitude
2. Fuel Level
3. Temperature
4. Oxygen Level
5. Power Level
6. Pressure
7. Speed
8. Crew Size

Each parameter contributes to a weighted risk score

⚖️

Risk Calculation System

Rule-based scoring + Neural Network prediction

Example Rules:
Fuel < 20% → 90% risk
Altitude < 200km → 85% risk

Final output: 4 risk levels

Low
Moderate
High
Critical
